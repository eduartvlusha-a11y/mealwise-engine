MealWise Engine

Deterministic nutrition & grocery optimization system designed to generate structured weekly meal plans based on budget, health goals, and constraints.

What it does

  • Calculates BMR, TDEE, calories and protein
  • Generates weekly meal plans
  • Builds grocery lists with cost estimation
  • Applies strict dietary constraints

Tech Stack

  • NestJS backend
  • Prisma ORM
  • Deterministic engine logic

Use Cases

  • Meal planning apps
  • Health platforms
  • Budget nutrition tools
